Our Story

Retro Closet 868 was born from a simple obsession: finding the jerseys that tell the real story of football.

Not replicas. Not knock-offs. The real kits — the ones worn during title wins, World Cup glories, and iconic moments that live forever in the game.

We started with a wardrobe full of jerseys, a deep love for the culture, and a belief that Trinidad & Tobago deserved access to the best of football fashion — without having to travel abroad or pay inflated prices.

Today, Retro Closet 868 serves football lovers across TT, bringing them the most iconic jerseys in the game — from Ronaldo's treble season at United to Mbappe's Madrid debut kit.

"Every jersey has a story. We just make it yours."

We carefully curate each piece in our collection — if it doesn't have history, heat, or culture behind it, it doesn't make the cut.
